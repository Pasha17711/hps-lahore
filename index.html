Our Story
"Quality education is every child's right."

HPS was established inspired by the philosophy of Shaheed-e-Pakistan Hakim Muhammad Said, the same tradition behind Hamdard Foundation and Madinat-ul-Hikma. That vision still shapes how HPS teaches today: rigorous academics under the Cambridge and Matric curricula, delivered without putting quality education out of reach.
