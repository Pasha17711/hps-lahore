Welcome to HPS
"Quality education is every child's right."

HPS was established in 1999 in Model Town, Lahore, inspired by the philosophy of Shaheed-e-Pakistan Hakim Muhammad Said. That founding idea, that quality education shouldn't be out of reach, still shapes how the school teaches today, across the Cambridge and Matric curricula.
