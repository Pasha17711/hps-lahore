## Flagged Conflicts (not silently resolved)
1. **"100 years of legacy" vs. founded 1999.** One Instagram admissions ad states "For over 100
   years, [the school] has been shaping young minds... Where 100 Years of Legacy Meets World-Class
   Education," referencing the broader Hamdard Foundation's history. The school's own Instagram
   bio, separately, states it was established in 1999. These are two different timelines from two
   different official sources. The current site uses only the 1999 founding date and does **not**
   use the "100 years" claim, since it isn't clear whether that refers to the school itself or the
   wider foundation it's associated with. **Needs a decision from the school on which framing they
   want used, if either.**
2. **"Most affordable fees in the area."** This is the school's own marketing language from its
   official Instagram admissions ad, not an independently verifiable fact. It's a real quote from
   a real, official source, but it's a subjective claim, not a benchmarked one. Currently kept on
   the admissions page as the school's own stated position, not presented as a verified ranking.
